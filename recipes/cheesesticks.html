Cheese Sticks

Description

These cheesy, buttery, crunchy sticks are a great appetizer for any party. They're easy to make and can be made ahead of time. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping.

The wider the strips, the more bread-like the cheese sticks will be. The tighter you twist them, the more dense and crisp they will be. A loose twist will result in a softer, breadier stick.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Colby cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Asiago cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Havarti cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Asiago cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Havarti cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Colby cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Asiago cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Havarti cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Colby cheese

Steps/Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese and Parmesan cheese until smooth. Stir in mozzarella cheese, cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, Colby cheese, Swiss cheese, Asiago cheese, provolone cheese, Romano cheese, Gruyere cheese, Havarti cheese, sharp cheddar cheese, sharp provolone cheese, sharp Asiago cheese, sharp Romano cheese, sharp Gruyere cheese, sharp Havarti cheese, sharp Swiss cheese, sharp Colby cheese, sharp Monterey Jack cheese, sharp mozzarella cheese, and sharp Parmesan cheese. Mix until well blended.
  4. Spread mixture onto prepared baking sheet. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  5. Using a pizza cutter, cut cheese mixture into strips. Twist strips together and serve.
