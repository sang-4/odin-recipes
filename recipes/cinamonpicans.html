Cinnamon Pecans

Description

Crunching on these pecans, coated in cinnamon, cayenne, and sugar, satisfies pre-meal cravings. Better yet, you can bring a jar of this appetizer as a gift to your host, as our home cooks point out. Store in an airtight container for up to two weeks in the pantry or a month in the freezer.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pecan halves

Steps/Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Combine sugar, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, and salt in a bowl. Stir in pecans until evenly coated.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until pecans are toasted and sugar is melted, about 10 minutes. Stir pecans every 2 minutes to prevent burning.
